Immersive Technologies in Education: Challenges

Infrastructure Constraints
  • High initial equipment investment
  • Need for specialised technical support
  • Physical space considerations
  • Bandwidth and connectivity requirements
User Experience
  • Simulator sickness (session length/frequency)
  • Ergonomic concerns (extended usage)
  • Cognitive overload (complex simulations)
  • Accessibility for users with impairments
Teacher Capacity
  • Need for specialised technological training
  • Time demands for lesson preparation
  • Resistance to pedagogical change
  • Limited ongoing professional development
Evaluation Frameworks
  • Lack of standardised assessment methods
  • Lack of long-term impact assessment
  • Limited integration with existing systems
  • Need for new Learning Analytics approaches
