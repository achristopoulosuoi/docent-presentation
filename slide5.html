Immersive Technologies in Education: Benefits

Enhanced Engagement
  • Increases intrinsic motivation
  • Extends attention span
  • Creates emotional investment
  • Promotes active participation
Authentic Learning
  • Transforms abstract concepts
  • Promotes experiential knowledge
  • Enables iterative practice & refinement
  • Builds procedural memory
Social Aspect
  • Fosters global collaboration
  • Facilitates role-based learning
  • Enables collaborative problem-solving
  • Creates shared understanding
Resource Optimisation
  • Creates scalable environments
  • Provides access to expensive equipment
  • Enables reuse of resources
  • Promotes sustainable practices
